Old Firefox Icon:

The above image is of the previous Firefox icon. In 2019, Firefox released a new, slick icon. The new icon makes the fox less prominent by morphing it more into a 'swoosh'. The change was partly made to modernize their brand, but also to create an overarching identity for all Firefox products.

Benefits of change:

  • modern design
  • create overarching brand theme
  • media will report on change
